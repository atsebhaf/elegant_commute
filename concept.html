Concept

Tagline

A tech based approach to reducing the hustle involved while commuting using public means.

Project Description:

This project attempts to establish the common and frequent challenges faced by commuters using public transport. Once establish, a technological approach will be used in mitigating some of the identified challenges and in the end, improve the user experience of commuting from one point to another.

Design Questions:

  • How can technology be used to improve public transportation?

User Research Questions:

  • Who are the routine users of public transport?
  • What challenges do these routine users currently face during their commute?
  • When is the most prevalent time that these commuting challenges are witnessed?
  • Which zones pose the most troubling times during a commute?

Target Users

  • Regular users of public buses
  • Regular users of trains
  • Regular taxi users
